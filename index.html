Textbooks

Compare Prices Online Textbook Pick-Up ServiceBook RentalsPurchase e-Books Online

The Langara Bookstore offers all the official textbooks and course materials as required by your instructor. You can buy them in store, or purchase or access them online, or rent them. Check out your various options below.

TEXTBOOKS

  • USED—save up to 25% over new.
  • RENTAL—save up to 50% over new.
  • DIGITAL—save up to 30% over new.
  • BUY BACK – Students can buy new and then return for up to 50% Cash back at the end of exams during Buy Back.
  • COMPARE PRICES / PRICE MATCHING —Order through our Compare Site, a gateway to take advantage of our competitors’ selection and pricing AND still contribute to your campus community. Bookstore now also price matches Amazon.ca. 

Keep it on Campus 

The Bookstore gives any surplus dollars back to the college, and all course materials are available from the bookstore at reasonable prices. Shop at the Langara Bookstore and know that your shopping dollars stay on campus to help fund services for students, new classroom technologies, and other great initiatives.

Compare prices online to find the best price for your textbooks.

Check out Langara's Textbook Compare Tool on the Bookstore website or myLangara. This service allows you to view your booklist and compare Langara Bookstore prices alongside other online retailers so you don’t have to, and it shows that our prices are very competitive. 

Planning on buying from Amazon.ca? We now offer In-store Price-Matching for new textbooks on Amazon.ca.

For other cost-saving options, we stock used textbooks, offer textbook rentals, and access codes & eTexts.

Get your personalized course materials list

We’ve created the Find My Textbooks Tool which you can use to view, print, and shop for your personal booklist/course materials for this semester. 
The Find My Textbooks Tool is in myLangara.  Click the MyCourses tab and the Find My Textbooks Tool is located on the right hand side.  Click Show my book list to view your personalized book list!

Choose pick-up at Langara Bookstore or delivery when you shop online

Choose the Textbook Pick-Up Service for all online purchases through the Bookstore to avoid long lineups! Or choose to have them delivered to your door.

  • Pick-Up Service is $4.00.  We'll pick and pack your textbooks for you and you can bypass the start-of-term line when you come in to pick up your books!
  • Textbook Delivery Service within BC is $9.99
  • Shipping is free for orders over $300.00.
